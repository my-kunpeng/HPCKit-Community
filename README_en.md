## 2. About the Community

### 2.1 Community Governance Architecture

The HPCKit community adopts a layered collaborative governance model as follows:

- [Technical Steering Committee (TSC)](governance/TSC/README.md): responsible for technical vision and strategic decision-making.
- [Special Interest Groups (SIGs)](sig): focused on specific technical domains or the development of common capabilities.

### 2.2 Community Governance Charter

For details about the community governance charter, see the following documents:

- [TSC Governance Charter](governance/tsc-governance.md)
- [SIG Governance Charter](governance/sig-governance.md)
- [Community Role Definitions and Promotion Mechanism](governance/role-definition-and-promotion-mechanism.md)

## 3. Code of Conduct

Before contributing to the community, please carefully read and comply with the [Code of Conduct](contributor/code-of-conduct.md). All activities within the HPCKit community (including but not limited to posting comments, submitting issues, pull requests, and documents, etc.) must comply with this code of conduct.

## 4. Contributing

- [Signing the CLA](https://clasign.osinfra.cn/sign/69ca19493107795d582d07fc): Sign the individual or corporate Contributor License Agreement (CLA).
- [Basic contributions](contributor/basic-contribution.md): Includes participating in community meetings, engaging in email discussions, submitting issues, handling issues, and submitting pull requests.
- [Advanced contributions](contributor/advanced-contribution.md): Includes creating SIGs, becoming a core contributor, organizing meetings, creating repositories, introducing open-source software, and releasing new versions or repositories.

## 5. Contact Us

- [Mailing list subscription](https://mailweb.hpckit.osinfra.cn/postorius/lists): Select the organization (such as TSC or SIG) you want to subscribe to and fill in the required information to subscribe to the email notifications (including meeting notices, meeting minutes, and technical discussions). If you have specific requests or questions for an organization, you may also contact them via email.
